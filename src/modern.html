The Internet Today

The Internet has grown to spread it's roots across the most human aspects of life, influencing how we seek healthcare, the dissemination and proliferation of the arts, and even setting up the first meeting between potential future spouses. Any job, no matter how white collar, has been put at risk of replacement with the advancement of software, and blue collar jobs have had one foot out the door since the developement of robotic arms beginning in the early 1960's.
